# Nanabozho

**Pronunciation:** nah-nah-BOH-zhoh

Nanabozho is the studio behind **Everden**.

## Meaning

In Ojibwe tradition, Nanabozho is a creator, teacher, and culture hero — a trickster who overcomes impossible odds through **intelligence, creativity, and perseverance**, not brute strength.

That is how we build games.

## Studio Values

| Value | In practice |
|-------|-------------|
| Ingenuity over scale | Small team, clever systems, no bloat |
| Creativity over brute force | Biology-driven design, not stat spreadsheets pasted on fur |
| Perseverance over shortcuts | Long-term architecture; extend, don't rewrite |
| Believability first | The world exists without the player |

## Relationship to Everden

- **Nanabozho** makes the game.
- **Everden** is the world.

Credits, branding, and public-facing identity use Nanabozho. The game title remains Everden.
