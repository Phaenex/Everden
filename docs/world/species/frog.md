# Frog Folk

**Combat role:** Mobile / scout  
**Homeland affinity:** Open water, lily platforms, swift currents

## Biology

- Moist skin; must stay damp or overheat in dry sun
- Powerful hind legs — leap 3× body length
- Stereoscopic vision; excellent motion detection
- Tongue strike for prey capture (short range, fast)
- Eggs laid in water; tadpole stage 2–3 years

## Culture

### Build
Stilt platforms, rope bridges, floating market stalls. Structures are light and repositionable after floods.

### Fight
Hit-and-run. Leap over obstacles. Ambush from water. Avoid prolonged melee.

### Hunt / Farm
Insect farming, algae ponds, spear-fishing. No large livestock.

### Worship
**The First Ripple** — creation myth: world began when something dropped into still water. Ceremonies involve synchronized jumping to send ripples across a pond.

### Travel
Boats, leaps between platforms, swim. Poor overland endurance in dry heat.

### Government
Merchant guilds hold informal power; council seats earned by trade revenue and public works.

### Family
Communal tadpole nurseries in shared ponds. Adults contribute by rotation.

### Death
Bodies returned to water; belief that ripples carry memory downstream.
