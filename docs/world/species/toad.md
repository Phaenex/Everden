# Toad Folk

**Combat role:** Control / debuff  
**Homeland affinity:** Burrows, humid hollows, ferry banks

## Biology

- Warty, thicker skin than frogs — better desiccation resistance
- Parotoid glands produce bufotoxin (defensive, medicinal when diluted)
- Shorter legs, stronger forelimbs for digging
- Excellent night vision
- Long lifespan (40–60 years); slow to anger, slow to forgive

## Culture

### Build
Burrow-shops with clay facades. Sunken courtyards trap humidity. Doors are round, low.

### Fight
Territorial defense. Poison, fear croaks, choke points. Rarely pursue fleeing enemies.

### Hunt / Farm
Root vegetables in shaded gardens. Insect traps. Fermentation (tonics, poisons, medicines — same tools, different doses).

### Worship
**The Deep Croak** — belief that the earth remembers every sound made upon it. Temples are acoustic chambers underground.

### Travel
Walk, burrow-shortcuts in familiar territory. Ferry operation is a toad specialty (patient, steady).

### Government
Elders by age; disputes settled by "three nights of listening" before verdict.

### Family
Small clutches; parents guard burrow for decades. Children inherit craft.

### Death
Buried in earth; a croak is sounded at dawn for seven days so the earth remembers.
