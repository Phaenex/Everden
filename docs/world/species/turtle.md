# Turtle Folk

**Combat role:** Tank  
**Homeland affinity:** Stone foundations, river bends, flood walls

## Biology

- Bony shell fused to spine — natural armor
- Can withdraw limbs for protection (immobile but heavily defended)
- Slow metabolism; can hold breath underwater for hours
- Live 80–150 years; memory spans generations
- Strong jaws; herbivorous primary diet

## Culture

### Build
Domed masonry, shell-inspired curves, lowest center of gravity. Buildings meant to survive floods.

### Fight
Shield walls, choke points, never retreat from assigned ground. Siege specialists.

### Hunt / Farm
Aquatic plants, river algae farms, slow-growing orchards on raised berms.

### Worship
**The Long Shell** — time is a circle; the world carries its history on its back. Temples are stone domes with whisper galleries.

### Travel
Boats (they ballast well), plodding overland. Build the roads others use.

### Government
Council of Shells — turtle elders hold plurality; frogs and toads hold minority seats by charter.

### Family
Few offspring; communal raising in shell-halls. Elders teach masonry and law.

### Death
Shells become foundation stones for public works. "Returned to the wall."
