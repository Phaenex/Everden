# Faction — Croakend Artisans

**Leader:** Marta Clayhollow  
**Species lean:** Toad  
**Base:** Croakend, Lilypond

## Goals

- Stay neutral in levy dispute (sell to both sides)
- Legalize medicinal toxin trade without frog merchant middlemen
- Preserve burrow property rights during flood construction

## Grievances

- Ferry tolls hurt night market traffic
- Masons' heavy equipment cracks burrow roofs

## Methods

Quiet lobbying, potion gifts, information brokering

## Relationship to Player

Information source. Can broker compromise endings.

## World State Flags

- `toxin_trade_legalized` → croakend prices drop, unique items unlock
- `burrow_compensation` → toads support player in council vote
