# Faction — Vale Wanderers

**Leader:** none formal — family clans, healers hold informal authority  
**Species lean:** Vole  
**Base:** Meadow burrows east of Reedwater Basin (not yet a physical location in-game)

## Goals

- Stay entirely out of the levy dispute — frog, toad, or shell, doesn't matter
- Keep trade routes open between the basin and the upland forest
- Protect burrow clans from being drawn into basin politics

## Grievances

- Basin factions assume neutrality means indifference
- Flooding from the levy fight reaches meadow burrows too, but no one asks the voles about it

## Methods

Trade, healing, gossip-for-goods. No levers of power in the basin — deliberately.

## Relationship to Player

Currently a single NPC (Sable Meadowrun, Lilymarket). Not yet load-bearing in any quest — a neutral third voice for players who want basin politics from an outsider's view. See `docs/world/species/vole.md` for the species writeup this faction is built on.

## World State Flags

None yet — first candidate for the T11 second-settlement pass if the vale wanderers get an actual location.
