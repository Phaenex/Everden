# Faction — Lilymarket Traders

**Leader:** Pip Marshwick (de facto)  
**Species lean:** Frog  
**Base:** Lilymarket, Lilypond

## Goals

- Block or modify north levy to prevent Lilymarket flooding
- Lower ferry tolls set by toad operators
- Expand floating stall licenses

## Grievances

- Mudwall levy channels water toward their district
- Turtle council majority stalls stall expansion votes

## Methods

Boycotts, rumor campaigns, leap-protests on council steps

## Relationship to Player

Quest giver faction. Friendly by default. Expects player to investigate levy impact honestly.

## World State Flags

- `market_protected` → traders friendly, masons angry
- `ferry_deal_brokered` → ferry toll reduced, toads neutral
