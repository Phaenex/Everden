# Faction — Mudwall Masons

**Leader:** Elder Shellen Domet  
**Species lean:** Turtle  
**Base:** Mudwall district, Lilypond

## Goals

- Complete the north levy before next flood season
- Secure council funding for stone imports from upland quarry
- Establish precedent: flood defense overrides downstream complaints

## Grievances

- Frog merchants refuse to pay flood tax share
- "Downstream" frogs still blame turtles for the Great Spill

## Methods

Political pressure, slow strikes (masonry stops), elder testimony

## Relationship to Player

Recruitable ally if player supports levy. Antagonistic if player sabotages construction.

## World State Flags

- `levy_supported` → masons friendly, lilymarket prices rise
- `levy_opposed` → masons hostile, flood risk event in spring
