# Antagonist — Foreman Kess "Hardback" Ridge

**Species:** Turtle  
**Role:** Not evil — ideological opponent

## Position

Believes flood walls are the only morality that matters. Lost family in a pre-Charter flood. Will complete the north levy with or without council approval.

## Goals

- Finish levy by force if vote fails
- Prove downstream frogs exaggerate flood risk
- Retire having "fixed" the Great Spill's lesson

## Followers

A small crew of masons who trust his engineering over politics

## Weakness

Genuinely cannot understand why frogs won't accept mathematical flood models. Not a listener.

## Internal Conflict

Respects Elder Domet but considers her too slow. May act alone if player stalls quest.

## If Player Understands Him

He'll explain the night of the Great Spill — he was a tadpole-equivalent child; watched water rise. Player can still oppose him without dismissing his fear.

## Outcomes

| Player action | Result |
|---------------|--------|
| Expose levy flaw | Kess halts work, joins redesign |
| Support levy | Kess completes; Lilymarket floods in spring |
| Sabotage levy | Kess becomes hostile; combat or arrest path |
| Broker compromise | Modified levy + frog flood platforms; best ending |

## World State Flags

- `kess_stopped`, `kess_completed`, `kess_compromise`
