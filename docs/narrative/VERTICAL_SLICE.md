# Vertical Slice — Quest Chain

**Title:** What the Water Remembers  
**Type:** Investigation → diplomacy → consequence  
**Region:** Reedwater Basin / Lilypond

## Premise

Frog merchants report rising water in Lilymarket cellars. Turtle masons deny the north levy is responsible. Council vote in 7 game days. Someone is lying — or the math is wrong.

## Stages

### 1. Ripples in the Cellar
- **Trigger:** Enter Lilypond, talk to Pip Marshwick
- **Objective:** Examine flooded cellar, collect waterline marks
- **Learn:** Flood pattern inconsistent with natural rain

### 2. The Mason's Measure
- **Trigger:** Show evidence to Elder Domet OR investigate levy site directly
- **Objective:** Obtain levy plans (persuasion, stealth, or council permit)
- **Learn:** Levy channels 30% more flow south than charter allows

### 3. Chapel Murals
- **Trigger:** Visit Sunken Chapel
- **Objective:** Examine murals (environmental storytelling)
- **Learn:** Original settlement designed oxbow overflow channels — now blocked

### 4. Ferryman's Truth
- **Trigger:** Talk to Grizz at Ferryman's Rest at night
- **Objective:** Cross-reference current readings with ferry depth marks
- **Learn:** Kess may be working off outdated flood tables

### 5. Council of Shells
- **Trigger:** Return to Lilypond with evidence; vote imminent
- **Objective:** Present findings; player chooses outcome

## Player Choices (Consequential)

| Choice | Flag | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| Expose Kess publicly | `kess_stopped` | Masons angry; market saved; redesign quest hook |
| Back masons | `levy_supported` | Market flooded spring; stone trade booms |
| Broker compromise | `kess_compromise` | Modified levy + frog platforms; all factions neutral-positive |
| Sell evidence to highest bidder | `evidence_sold` | Short-term gold; all factions distrust player |
| Do nothing | `vote_default` | Kess completes levy; worst flood outcome |

## Who Benefits / Suffers

| Outcome | Benefits | Suffers |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Compromise | Whole basin | Nobody fully wins |
| Stop Kess | Lilymarket | Upland stone trade delayed |
| Support levy | Mudwall, upland | Lilymarket, ferries |
| Sell out | Player wallet | Everyone else |

## Combat Optional

Blackfen Outlet: poachers harassing Grizz's ferry. Demonstrates frog/toad/turtle roles. Not required for quest completion.

## NPC Schedule Hooks

- Pip at market 8–18
- Domet at council shell 10–14
- Kess at levy site 6–20
- Grizz at ferry 0–6 and 18–24
